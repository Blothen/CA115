About Us DigiCare

A dynamic team of six associates united to pioneer the development of preventative healthcare wearables and an accompanying app. Our vision crystallized around the concept of healthcare wearables empowered by machine learning algorithms, designed to continuously monitor an individual's baseline health metrics and proactively alert healthcare professionals to potential emergent health issues.

Jack and James bring to the table their entrepreneurial mindset, driving our vision forward with unwavering determination. Ciaran and Ramjam, our seasoned programmers, leverage their expertise in app development to engineer innovative solutions. Meanwhile, Shane and Jingyuan excel in strategic planning and goal setting, ensuring our trajectory remains focused and purposeful.

Recognizing a glaring gap in the market where existing products fell short of truly assisting individuals, we, at DigiCare, resolved to harness our collective knowledge and skill sets to deliver a transformative service—one that not only aids but safeguards our customers.
